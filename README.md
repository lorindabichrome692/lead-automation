## 🖥️ What this app does

lead-automation helps you capture new leads, store them in Airtable, and send follow-up emails with less manual work. It uses FastAPI, Claude AI, Resend, and Streamlit to keep the process simple from start to finish.

Use it to:

- Save lead details in one place
- Sort and review incoming leads
- Send follow-up emails based on lead data
- Keep your lead list up to date
- Track outreach without spreadsheets scattered everywhere

## 🗂️ How the workflow works

The app follows a simple flow:

1. A lead comes in through a form or import.
2. The app sends the lead data to Airtable.
3. Claude AI helps shape the follow-up message.
4. Resend sends the email.
5. The app records what happened so you can review it later.

This keeps your lead process in one path instead of spread across many tools.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

If the app does not open:

- Make sure the download finished
- Try running it again as an administrator
- Check that Windows did not block the file
- Make sure you extracted the .zip file first

If leads do not save:

- Check your Airtable details
- Make sure your internet connection works
- Confirm that the base and table names match what the app expects

If emails do not send:

- Check your Resend key
- Make sure the sender email is approved
- Confirm that the email address is valid

If Claude AI does not respond:

- Check your Anthropic key
- Make sure your account has access
- Review any rate limits on your account
